Our Product Range

Mill Gear Boxes

“Kathuria” is renowned for the design and manufacture of gearing solutions. We work closely with our customers to provide a solution to meet their specific application needs. Today, our industrial gear units are especially valued by our customers due to their high level of accuracy, their quality and their attractive price-performance ratio.

Our selection of universal gear unit designs is perfectly supplemented by a wide range of standard options. For this reason, our portfolio provides a virtually unlimited variety of design options. The gear units compact design, the choice between horizontal and vertical installation and the simple attachment of auxiliary components providing the maximum flexibility when designing a plant.

Where customers require a custom designed gearbox, be it due to space constraints, environment or specification, Kathuria’s Gears has extensive expertise and knowledge across many sectors and can therefore provide a unique design & manufacturing service supported by full project management.

Our product range mainly consists of:

Helical Gears

Kathuria manufactures high quality, high specification helical and spur gearboxes using leading edge gear technology. Our dedicated gear manufacturing machine shop provides the facilities necessary to manufacture either volume or single components for both precision and commercial sectors.

Our experienced Engineering Department can advise on all areas of gear design - gear blank materials, heat treatments, surface treatments and full gear tooth geometry. Using gear design software our engineering team are able to maximise gear performance and therefore provide a unique gear design and development service.

    Salient features are:
  • High accuracy finish hobbed or ground gear teeth
  • Helical / Spur gears
  • Volume or small batch production
  • Custom tool profiles
  • Gear grinding up to 1200 mm diameter and gear face widths up to 350 mm
  • Optimising gear performance
Planetary Gears

“Kathuria’s” Planetary gearheads are high-precision, motion-control devices that generate substantial torque for their size, have high torsional stiffness, and low backlash — making them suited for wide-ranging tasks.

Our continuous R&D and experience of more than two decades enabled us to cater the growing needs and requirements of various industrial applications by wide range of modular designed Planetary Gearboxes.

    Salient features are:
  • High Efficiency
  • High Torque to weight ratio (Compact Design)
  • Wide Range of Gear Ratios
  • High grade casing for protection from hazardous contents
Bevel Gears

“Kathuria’s” bevel gearboxes are the result of an accurate and modern design technology and of the precise calculation of each single component and its assembling, which allows ensuring the declared performances with a high safety factor. Their development and production are carried out following the quality management procedures of Kathuria, a certified company according to ISO 9001:2015 norms.

The accurate and consolidated manufacturing technology allows to produce bevel gears that able to work with low noise level and with high efficiency.

    Salient features are:
  • High performances and compact dimensions
  • Tapered roller bearings standard on all solid shafts and hollow output shaft
  • Ball bearings on hollow input shafts for motor flange execution.
  • Low noise level and high efficiency
  • High quality alloy steel
  • Longer service life
